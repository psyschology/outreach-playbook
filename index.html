This 3-hour schedule is a sprint, not a marathon. It is designed to be followed without distraction to maximize your output and your commission.

9:00 AM - 9:45 AM (45 Minutes)

Update their status in the Google Sheet to "DM Sent". You must be fast and accurate.

4

11:45 AM - 12:00 PM (15 Minutes)

Task 4: Final Report & Handoff

Your mission is complete. Double-check your Google Sheet for accuracy. All 40 prospects must be logged with their final status. If you have any positive replies, **immediately** take a screenshot and post it in our shared Slack channel with the message "New Positive Reply".
