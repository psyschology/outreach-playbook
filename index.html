Lead Generation Specialist

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

Welcome, Somborna. This document is your complete, step-by-step guide to your daily work.

Your Main Goal at Jetweb Labs

Your job is the most important first step for our company's growth. Your one and only goal is to **find businesses on Instagram that need our help and start a friendly conversation with them.** A successful day for you is getting a positive reply. That reply becomes a potential client for the company.

Who to Contact: The Perfect Customer

We are looking for small businesses in India who are trying to sell online but are doing it badly. Your job is to find accounts that have these specific problems:

Look for these problems (The Goldmines):

  • Their bio says **"DM to order"** or **"WhatsApp to order"**. This is your #1 target.
  • They have **NO website link** in their bio.
  • The website link in their bio is broken, slow, or looks very old and unprofessional.

These are also good signs:

  • They post new content regularly.
  • They sell a real product (clothes, cakes, jewelry) or service (cafe, salon).
  • Their product photos are good.

Your Minute-by-Minute Daily Schedule (Mon-Fri)

This 3.5-hour schedule is intense and packed. It requires your full focus. Following this schedule is how you will meet your targets and earn your commission.

1

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM (60 Minutes)

Task 1: Prospecting (Build Your Hit List)

Open Instagram. Your only goal is to find businesses with the problems listed above. Use the hashtags provided below. Find **30** new business accounts and add their Instagram Handle (e.g., @priyas_cakes) to a new column in your Google Sheet. **Do not contact them yet.** Just build the list.

2

10:00 AM - 11:00 AM (60 Minutes)

Task 2: The "Engage First" Protocol

From your list of 30, pick the best 25. Now, visit each of their profiles. For each one, you must do two things: **1. Follow them. 2. Leave one high-quality, genuine comment** on their most recent post. This proves you are a human. Update their status in the Google Sheet to "Engaged".

3

11:00 AM - 12:15 PM (75 Minutes)

Task 3: Send Personalized Messages

From your list of 25 you engaged with, pick the top 20. Now is the time to send a Direct Message (DM). Go to the "Script Library" section below, choose the right script for the business type, and **personalize it using the AI tool.** This is the most important part of your job. Update their status in the Google Sheet to "DM Sent".

4

12:15 PM - 12:30 PM (15 Minutes)

Task 4: Final Report

Double-check your Google Sheet. Make sure all statuses are correct. If you received any positive replies, **immediately** take a screenshot and send it to your manager via Slack with the message "New Positive Reply". Your work for the day is complete.

Do NOT send the same message to everyone. A personalized message gets a reply. A generic one is deleted. Use these tools to win.

Here is the information about the business:
- Business Name: [PASTE BUSINESS NAME HERE]
- Business Type: [e.g., Home Baker, Fashion Boutique, Jewelry Store]
- Their Instagram Bio: "[PASTE THEIR BIO HERE]"

Based on this information, give me 3 options for a powerful, friendly, and highly specific opening line for my DM. The line should compliment their work and mention something unique I could only know by looking at their profile. Start the message with "Hi [Business Name]," and my name is Somborna.

After you get your personalized opening line from the AI, copy and paste the rest of the message from the correct template below.

Bronze Tier: 10% Commission

For projects up to ₹14,999. (Example: a ₹4,999 project earns you a **₹500 bonus**).

Silver Tier: 12% Commission

For projects from ₹15,000 to ₹49,999. (Example: a ₹20,000 project earns you a **₹2,400 bonus**).

Gold Tier: 15% Commission

For projects ₹50,000 and above. (Example: a ₹1,00,000 project earns you a **₹15,000 bonus**).

How a "Closed Deal" is Counted:

A deal is officially "closed" and your commission is earned when:
1. You find the lead and get the first positive reply.
2. The management team closes the deal.
3. The new client has **paid their first invoice** to the company.
Once the payment is confirmed, your commission will be marked as "Earned" and paid with your next monthly salary.
